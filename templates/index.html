Obesity Level Estimator

Estimate your obesity level based on your eating habits and physical condition.

About this Project

This project was created using a dataset for the estimation of obesity levels based on eating habits and physical conditions from individuals in Mexico, Peru, and Colombia. The dataset, originally containing 2,111 records, was synthetically improved to 20.8k entries for a Kaggle competition, and I achieved an accuracy of over 90%. This tool allows you to fill in the same survey used in the research and provides an obesity level prediction based on machine learning.

World Exploration of Obesity

Obesity is a global health issue affecting millions, and understanding your risk level can help you take preventive action. Our model can categorize your obesity level and provide medical advice based on your input. It's a great way for anyone to be more conscious of their health.

Fun Facts about Obesity

  • Obesity is one of the leading risk factors for early death
  • Globally, more people are obese than underweight, a trend observed in every region except sub-Saharan Africa and Asia.
  • Increased urbanization and sedentary lifestyles have contributed to the rise of obesity worldwide.
